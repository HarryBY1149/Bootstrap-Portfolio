About Me

My name is Harry Young and I am a fledgling full stack web developer. I studied coding at the Penn LPS coding bootcamp. I am an avid reader, musician, cook and gamer.

I was a restaurant professional for twelve years before making the leap into the wonderful world of coding. Those twelve years taught me a number of skills that translate well to my new vocation, problem solving chief among them. I bring the focus, dedication, and off the cuff creativity of the kitchen to every project I work on.

Some of my favorite dishes to prepare include: Fettucine Alfredo with Chicken and Brocolli; Grilled Porkchops with Peach/Corn Succotash and a Cider Glaze; Molé Pulled Pork Cubanos; Mexican Street Corn; Risotto Del Mare; Flank Steak Pallella; Curried Squash and Pole Beans over Mashed Yuca.
